Introduction

Lord Rama is a central figure in Hindu mythology and is revered as the seventh avatar of Lord Vishnu. The epic Ramayana, attributed to the sage Valmiki, narrates the life and adventures of Rama. Born to King Dasharatha and Queen Kaushalya, Rama exemplifies virtue, righteousness, and duty. His unwavering commitment to dharma (righteousness) is epitomized by his exile to the forest for 14 years to fulfill his father's vow.
